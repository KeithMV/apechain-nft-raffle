"Hi [Name], thanks for your interest in our NFT raffle platform. I'm going to show you exactly what your community would get - a complete, branded raffle system that's already live and working. Let me share my screen."

"This is our live platform with real users and real money. Everything you see here would be branded with your logo, colors, and domain name."

**Point out:**
- Professional design and branding
- Active raffles with real participants

**Navigate to dashboard/create sections**

**Demonstrate:**

### "Is it secure?"
"Absolutely. Smart contracts are unhackable - they handle all funds. The code is audited and already processing real transactions safely."

### "What if it breaks?"
"Smart contracts can't break - they're immutable. For the interface, we provide 24-hour support and monthly updates."

"Complete visual customization - your logo, colors, domain. It will look like you built it in-house."

### "What's the ongoing cost?"
"Just $850/month for support and updates. No server costs, no infrastructure fees. Most clients break even with 15-20 raffles per month."

## What NOT to Say
- Don't mention it's built on ApeChain specifically (focus on "blockchain")
- Don't get too technical unless they ask
- Don't compare to other raffle platforms
- Don't mention any current issues or limitations
- Don't oversell - let the demo speak for itself
