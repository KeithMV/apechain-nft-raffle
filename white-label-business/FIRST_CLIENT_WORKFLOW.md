# First Client Transaction Workflow

## Step 1: They Say "Yes, I Want It" 
**What you do:**
- Send simple contract/agreement (can be email-based initially)
- Request 50% upfront ($4,000)
- Get their branding assets (logo, colors, domain name)

## Step 2: Payment & Project Kickoff
**What you collect:**
- $4,000 payment (PayPal, wire, crypto)
- Logo files (PNG, SVG)
- Brand colors (hex codes)
- Domain name they want to use
- Any specific customization requests

## Step 3: Technical Work (Days 1-5)
**What you actually do:**

### Day 1-2: Code Customization
- Copy your raffle platform code
- Replace "ApeChain Raffles" with their brand name
- Update colors to match their brand
- Swap logo files
- Update contract addresses for their blockchain

### Day 3-4: Deployment
- Set up their domain/hosting
- Deploy smart contracts to their preferred blockchain
- Test everything works
- Set up admin access for them

### Day 5: Handover
- Send them admin login details
- Provide basic documentation
- Quick training call (30 minutes)

## Step 4: Final Payment & Go Live
**What happens:**
- They test and approve
- You collect remaining $4,000
- Platform goes live
- Set up monthly billing for $850

## Step 5: Ongoing Support
**Monthly tasks:**
- Answer questions (usually minimal)
- Push updates if needed
- Collect $850/month

## Real Time Investment
- **Setup work**: 2-3 days of actual coding
- **Most time**: Communication and coordination
- **Ongoing**: 1-2 hours per month per client

## What You DON'T Have To Do
- Build new features (use existing platform)
- Provide 24/7 support
- Handle their customer service
- Manage their raffles

**You're basically doing a "find and replace" on your existing code, then deploying it with their branding.**
