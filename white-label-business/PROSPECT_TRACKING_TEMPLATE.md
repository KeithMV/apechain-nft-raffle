# White Label Prospect Tracking Template

## Simple Google Sheets Setup (15 minutes)

### Column Headers:
| Company/Project | Contact Name | Email | Date Sent | Template Used | Status | Next Action | Notes |
|----------------|--------------|-------|-----------|---------------|--------|-------------|-------|
| CryptoKitties | John Smith | john@cryptokitties.com | 2024-01-15 | Template 1 | Sent | Follow up 1/22 | Active community |
| Local Crypto Meetup | Sarah Jones | sarah@meetup.com | 2024-01-15 | Template 2 | Replied | Demo call 1/18 | Interested! |

### Status Options:
- **Sent** - Email sent, waiting for response
- **Replied** - They responded (positive or negative)
- **Demo Scheduled** - Call booked
- **Proposal Sent** - Sent pricing/contract
- **Closed Won** - Signed contract
- **Closed Lost** - Said no
- **Follow Up** - Need to follow up

### Next Action Options:
- **Follow up [date]** - When to send follow-up
- **Demo call [date]** - Scheduled demo
- **Send proposal** - Need to send pricing
- **Contract review** - Waiting on contract
- **None** - No action needed

### Why Track This:
- See what's working (which templates get responses)
- Know when to follow up
- Track conversion rates
- Don't lose prospects in the cracks
- Measure progress toward goals

### Daily Habit (2 minutes):
- Add new prospects you email
- Update status when you get responses
- Check "Next Action" column for today's tasks

**This prevents you from forgetting to follow up and helps you see patterns in what works.**
